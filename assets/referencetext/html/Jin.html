JIN

Focus Point: Throat Chakra

Concept: Jin Is A Place Where We Observe The Universe And What Binds Every Part Of Us To Every Part Of Everything Else

Mantra Meaning: Conscious Experience Of The Fire That Everything Is Really Made Of

Possible Effects: Increases Telapathic Ability, Communication, Knowledge-ability By Getting Into Contact With The Substance That Everything And Everyone Is Made Of

Mantra

  • AGNAYA (FIRE OF)
  • YAN MAYA (WHAT EVERYTHING IS REALLY MADE OF)
  • SWAHA (CONSCIOUS EXPERIENCE OF)

Gods In Religion For Added Visualization


Shinto:

Sumiyoshi, a god known for his love of poetry and the process of purification.


Buddhists:

Sho Kanzeon, which is another name for Avalokitesvara, the Buddha of Compassion.

How It Works

The "Inner Bonds" Are The Energy Currents Inside You That Connect You With Your True Self. By Reaching Deep Inside You, Into The Place With No Words, You May Get In Contact With This Same Place In Others. By Doing This, It Can Help You Gain An Understanding Of Why People Do The Things They Do. If You Don't Judge What You Perceive, You Will Perceive It With More Clarity.

Focus Point

  • Throat Chakra

Mudra

This Mudra Keeps All Fingers Inside, Helping Us Get To In Contact With All The Inner Connections We Make, At The Level Of The Mind, The Heart As Well As The Spirit. This Is The Mudra That Gets Every Level In Touch With The Others So That A Global Understanding Of Every Situation Can Be Attained. JIN Affects Both Understanding And Expression Of Knowledge. The Inner Bonds Will Eventually Lead To The Experience Of Knowledge Without Words. The Deepest Knowledge Is Made Out Of Conceptual Thought, Un-Worded And Un-Labeled By Our Human Interpret.

Meaning

  • Explain Or Demonstrate (Explained Or Demonstrated to Ourselves From Spirit To Human. Knowledge Is Gained Through Inner Observation And Contemplation)

Concept

JIN Is A Place Of Knowledge And Expression. It Is A Point Of View From Which We Observe The Universe And Its Workings. It Binds Every Part Of us With Every Part Of Everything Else. It Is A Place Where Knowledge Is Beyond Understanding, And Understanding Is Made Possible Only With The Experience Of Truth

This Technique Calls For The Stillness Of Our Human So That Spirit Can Penetrate It, And In The Calm Of A Heavenly Bliss, We Awaken To The Supernatural Truth About Ourselves

For Listening

  1. As With RIN
    • Trust Yourself And Let Down Your Guard
    • Pay Attention To The Way You Listen And Grow From There
    • Listen To Everyone And Everything And Trust Yourself That, In The End, All Will Be Well
  2. As With KYO
    • Be Responsible For What You Are. Develop Discernment And A Sense Of Good Judgment That Will Allow You To Acquire Only That Knowledge Which Is Best For You
  3. As With TOH
    • Develop Tolerance For What You Hear, Listen With Care And Lower Your Defenses
    • Accepting What Is Said Is Not An Admission Or Approval Of What Is Said To You
    • Acceptance Allows You To Acknowledge What Is Said, At The Level It Is Said From, And Does Not Imply That You Must Become Or Believe What Is Said To You
  4. As With SHA
    • Believe Know And Understand That Knowledge Is Power
    • Do Not Attempt To Control The Exchange Of Knowledge Until It Is your Turn To Define The Exchange Of Knowledge
    • The Knowledge Will Flow Naturally In Whatever Direction It Must
    • When You Notice The Knowledge Leads Nowhere, Take Full Responsibility And Manifest A Return To Peaceful Communication
  5. As With KAI
    • Do Not Judge What You Hear
    • Practice Having A Compassionate Ear, And Listening With Love

For Speaking

  1. As With RIN
    • You Have The Right To Express Yourself, But This Does Not Mean That You Must Get Your Point Of View Across At Any Cost,It Simply Means That Your Spoken Words Have Value
    • DO NOT WASTE YOUR PRECIOUS SPEECH WHEN IT IS NOT BEING RECEIVED
  2. As With KYO
    • Take Responsibility For What You Say
    • Never Lie By Commission Or Omission; NEVER BE DECEITFUL
    • To Lie In Whatever Form Is To Condition Your Mind To Believe That What You Say Is False And Should Not Be Manifested
    • Always Speak The Truth, So That Your Mind Is Conditioned To Trusting That What You Say Is True
    • Say Words Only If They Are True, But If Words Are True They Must Be Spoken
  3. As With TOH
    • Be Humble In Choosing Your Words
    • Humility Means That You Are To Remain In Truth
    • IF SOMETHING SHOULD NOT BE SAID, THEN IT MUST NOT BE SAID And Vice Versa
    • To Become Wise, One Must Develop A Great Discernment, Especially In The Choice Of Words (Whether To Speak Them Or Not)
  4. As With SHA
    • Always Express Yourself In A Way That Will Lead To The Accomplishment Of Your Goal
    • Be Powerful In Your Speech
    • Your Words Must Always Be Clear And Must Always Represent That Which You Wish To Express
    • DO NOT HIDE BEHIND: Sarcasm, Lies, Manipulations or Controversial Arguments
  5. As With KAI
    • Speak Words Of Compassion And Love, Do Not Express Judgments And Destructive Criticism
    • Use Your Speech To Express Enlightenment And To Bring Enlightenment To Yourself
    • DO NOT Degrade Or Lower The Value Of Anyone, Of Anything, Or Any Experience
    • DO NOT Brag Or Complain About Anything
    • DO NOT Encourage The Ego To Play The Victim Game
