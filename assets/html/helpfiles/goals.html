I have built into the application a simple goal setting, achieving and tracking feature. I'll go over the basics to this in this short overview.

Setting Goals:

In order to begin to use this feature, you must first set a goal. To do this click 'NEW GOAL' on the bottom left of the application.

You will be presented with a dialog asking what your goal is:

  1. Number Of Goal Hours (Only whole numbers accepted right now)
  2. When You Want To Achieve That Goal (Click the little arrow to be presented with a little calendar date-select widget)

Rules for goals:

  1. Must not be 0 hrs.
  2. Goal hours you are setting must be greater than:
    1. You Current Practiced Hours (Displayed at the top of the 'New Goal' Dialog)
    2. Any other set goals
  3. Goal date must be after:
    1. Today's Date
    2. The latest date of all your goals

NOTE: I will check this when you click the 'SET THIS GOAL' button on the New Goal Dialog and display a warning if one of the rules isn't met (above)

Tracking Goals:

Once you have successfully set a goal, it will be tracked in the lower left hand side, and updated on program launch and after each session you complete.

Like so:

[current hours practiced] [%completed] [goal hours]

Calculating Practice Time Per Day To Reach The Goal (Goal Pacing)

Click The 'GOAL PACING' Button In The Lower Left (This Will Only Work If At Least 1 Goal is Set)

You Will Be Presented With A Dialog Asking How Many Days/wk You Want To Work Towards This Goal

After You Put In The Days A Week And Click Calculate, A Dialog Will Tell You How Many Hours (And Minutes) x Times A Week You Need To Work On That Goal

Viewing Active Goals

Click 'VIEW GOALS' In The Bottom Left. You Will Be Presented With A List Of All Active Goals

Viewing Inactive/Completed Goals

Click 'COMPLETED GOALS' In The Bottom Left. You Will Be Presented With A List Of All Completed Goals, With The Date You Completed Each Goal.
