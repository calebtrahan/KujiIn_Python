ZEN

Focus Point: Crown Chakra

Concept: The Human Completely Relents Itself To The Spirit With Only The Consciousness Aspect Of The Human Remaining Active

Mantra Meaning: I am the void and the light

Possible Effects: Completely Relenting Into Spirit Results In Englightenment, Completeness, Suggestive Invisibility

Steps

  1. Focus Only On Breathing For A Few Minutes
  2. Focus On Your Entire Physical Body For Another Few Minutes
  3. Focus On Your Feelings And Emotions For Another Few Minutes
  4. Concentrating Your Attention On The Process Of Feeling Your Mental Body (3rd Person Perspective Of The Mind Mechanisms) For A Few Minutes
  5. Finally, Pay Attention To Your Consciousness, Your Spirit Or Your Soul Or Whatever Notion Of Your Spiritual Existence You Can Grasp (Rather Than The Energy In Your Body)
  6. Then Slowly Drop Each Aspect Of The Kuji-In Technique, One After The Other

Visualization

Visualize an infinite vast light filling an infinite vast void and feel yourself being a part of it.

Gods In Religion For Added Visualization


Shinto:

Marishi-Ten, the guardian of warriors, said to cloak even to render invisible.


Buddhists:

Monju Bosatsu or Manjushri Bodhisattva, the Buddha of Wisdom.

Focus Point

  • Crown Chakra

Fixity Meditation

  1. Practice Fixity, Eyes Opened, No Mantra, From 5-45 Minutes. No Mind, No Word, No Image
  2. Maintain Yourself In This Spiritual Attitude As Long As Possible, While, At The Same Time, Remaining Totally Conscious Of Your Physical Reality
  3. Attempt To Maintain A Spiritual Attitude In All Of Your Actions, At Every Moment

Mudra

The Human Completely Relents Itself To The Spirit With The Only Aspect That Remaining Active In The Human Hand Is The Thumb Of Consciousness, So That It Can Touch The Consciousness Of The Spirit. Other Than That, The Left Hand Lies Over The Bed Laid By The Spirit. The Spirit Hand Receives The Resting Human Hand, To Care For It.

Meaning

  • In Front Or Before (In Reference To The Concept Of Obviousness. It Takes A Calm Mind To Perceive The Most Obvious Essence Of Reality In A World Filled With Fantasy, Dreams And Fears)
